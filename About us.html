About Us

We have a vast inventory of vintage timepieces from popular brands like Rolex, Omega, and Patek Philippe, as well as lesser-known brands and vintage watch models.

We also offer a range of vintage watches in different conditions, from restored and serviced to "as-is" condition, provide authentication services and offer warranties or return policies to ensure customer satisfaction.

And have experienced watchmakers who can perform a range of repairs and services, from simple battery replacements to more complex repairs like movement overhauls and restoration.
